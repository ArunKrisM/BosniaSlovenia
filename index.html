Road trip · 12 days

Bosnia & Slovenia

Sarajevo to Ljubljana by car, waterfalls and old towns along the way, lakes and caves at the end — with a fourth birthday somewhere in the middle.

21 Aug – 1 Sep 2026 2 adults + 1 toddler One car, Sarajevo → Ljubljana 🎂 Birthday: 27 Aug

Getting the car right

One car, dropped off in Ljubljana

Full control of timing, and the drive itself — river valleys, karst, forested hills — is worth doing rather than skipping. Costs more in one-way drop fees, but no backtracking and no extra airport day with a toddler.

Return in Sarajevo, fly, hire again

Cheaper car rental, but adds a flight leg (likely with a connection), a second rental, and a ~2.5hr backtrack to Sarajevo. Misses the Bosnia→Croatia scenery entirely.

Chosen approach: one car all the way, with the long Mostar→Ljubljana drive broken overnight at Plitvice Lakes.

Where to stay

Sarajevo · 2 nights

A few minutes' walk from the Latin Bridge, close to the Old Town, the cable car, and the carpet shop.

Jajce · 1 night

Small and walkable — any central guesthouse near the waterfall works.

Mostar · 1 night

Walking distance to the Old Bridge for the evening atmosphere.

Plitvice · 1 night

Near Entrance 1 or 2, to minimise the morning walk to the boardwalks.

Ljubljana · 5 nights

Central and walkable to the Triple Bridge and Old Town — makes for an easy birthday evening.

Before you go

Book the car early

International one-way availability and fees vary a lot between agencies — the sooner the better.

Reserve Jajce & Plitvice stays

Small towns, limited stock in August.

Pre-book timed entries

Postojna Cave and Vintgar Gorge both need timed tickets online in peak season.

Straža toboggan, tandem age

Worth confirming the tandem-sledge minimum age on the day — generally fine for a 4-year-old riding with an adult.
